☄️ COMETCRUSHER“In the void, silence is the only song left.”CometCrusher is a high-octane, vector-based reimagining of the classic 1979 Asteroids. You are a lone space warrior navigating the frozen graveyard of an ancient civilization. Shatter the debris, outrun the Sentinels, and listen to the echoes of a lost world.🚀 The TwistWhile the controls feel nostalgic, the environment is alive—and hostile.Frozen Rings: Circular gates that trigger "Memories." Passing through them reveals fragments of lore from the ancients.The Sentinels: Red diamond-shaped guardians that use Vector Seeking AI to hunt you down.Echo System: Text-based narrative triggers that fade into the background as you play.📐 Mathematical FoundationBuilt on pure Euclidean Geometry and Vector Physics, CometCrusher uses:Euler Integration: For fluid movement and momentum.Toroidal Space: A wrap-around screen logic that treats the play area as a continuous surface.Radial Collision: Precise hit detection using the distance formula:$$d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}$$🎮 How to PlayThrust: W or Up ArrowRotate: A/D or Left/Right ArrowsFire: SpacebarThe Goal: Score points by crushing comets ($100$ pts) and Sentinels ($1000$ pts). Fly through Frozen Rings to gain bonus points and uncover the story.🛠️ Tech StackEngine: Custom HTML5 Canvas / JavaScript (No external libraries).Physics: Vector-based inertia and friction.Render: Real-time procedural geometry for "frozen" comet shapes.“We built the rings to catch the light... now we only catch the frost.”
